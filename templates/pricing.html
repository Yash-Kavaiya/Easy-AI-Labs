Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our pricing and packages

What does an "interaction" mean in your pricing?

An interaction refers to a single conversation turn between a user and our AI system. For example, if a customer asks a question and receives a response, that counts as one interaction. Multiple messages within the same conversation would count as multiple interactions.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for our Starter and Professional plans. During the trial, you'll have access to all features included in the plan, with some usage limitations. For Enterprise solutions, we offer a proof of concept project to demonstrate the value before full implementation.

What happens if I exceed my monthly interaction limit?

If you exceed your monthly interaction limit, we'll automatically charge for additional interactions at a rate of $0.01 per interaction for the Starter plan and $0.008 per interaction for the Professional plan. We'll also notify you when you reach 80% of your limit so you can consider upgrading to a higher tier if needed.

Can I change plans or cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades and cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. There are no penalties for changing or canceling your subscription.

What kind of support is included with each plan?

The Starter plan includes email support with a 48-hour response time. The Professional plan offers priority email and phone support with a 24-hour response time. The Enterprise plan includes 24/7 premium support with a guaranteed 4-hour response time, a dedicated success manager, and regular performance reviews.

Do you offer custom solutions not listed here?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating custom AI solutions tailored to your specific business needs. Our Enterprise plan is fully customizable, and we can develop bespoke solutions that address your unique challenges. Contact our sales team to discuss your requirements and get a custom quote.
