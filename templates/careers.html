Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working at Easy AI Labs

Do you offer remote work options?

Yes, we offer flexible work arrangements including fully remote options for many roles. Some positions may require occasional presence in one of our offices or may be designated as in-office roles. Each job posting specifies the location requirements for that particular position.

What's the interview process like?

Our typical interview process includes an initial screening call, a role-specific assessment, and a series of interviews with team members and leadership. We aim to be respectful of your time while thoroughly evaluating mutual fit. The entire process usually takes 2-3 weeks, though we can expedite when needed.

Do I need AI experience to work at Easy AI Labs?

It depends on the role. For technical positions like AI Engineers, experience with AI technologies is typically required. However, for many non-technical roles, we value diverse backgrounds and are happy to help you learn about AI on the job. We look for candidates who are curious, adaptable, and excited about the potential of AI to transform customer experiences.

What's the company culture like?

We foster a culture of innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning. We value diverse perspectives, transparent communication, and a healthy work-life balance. Our team is passionate about solving complex problems with AI and delivering solutions that make a real impact for our clients. We work hard but also make time for team-building, celebration, and fun.

Do you offer internships or entry-level positions?

Yes, we offer internships and entry-level positions across various departments. We typically post internship opportunities for summer programs in January/February, and we have ongoing hiring for entry-level roles as needed. We're committed to developing talent and providing growth opportunities for those early in their careers.

Don't See the Right Fit?

We're always looking for talented individuals who are passionate about AI and customer experience. Send us your resume for future opportunities.
