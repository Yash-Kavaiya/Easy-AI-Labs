Financial Services Virtual Agents & Voice Bots

How NexusBank Transformed Customer Service with AI

Learn how a leading financial services provider achieved 85% self-service resolution, reduced call volumes by 45%, and improved customer satisfaction by 38% with our AI solutions.

Client Overview

NexusBank is a leading financial services provider with over 5 million customers and 500+ branches across North America. They offer a comprehensive range of banking, investment, and insurance products to both individual and business customers.

As a forward-thinking organization, NexusBank has been investing in digital transformation initiatives to enhance customer experience and streamline operations. However, their contact center operations were facing significant challenges with high call volumes, long wait times, and inconsistent customer experiences.

The Challenges

Long Wait Times

Customers were experiencing average wait times of 15+ minutes during peak hours, leading to frustration and negative feedback.

High Call Volumes

The contact center was handling over 2,000 calls daily, with 70% being routine inquiries that could potentially be automated.

Agent Burnout

Contact center agents were spending most of their time on repetitive tasks, leading to high turnover rates and increased training costs.

24/7 Support

Customers expected round-the-clock support, but staffing the contact center 24/7 was cost-prohibitive.

Inconsistent Experiences

Customer experiences varied widely depending on which agent handled their call, leading to inconsistent service quality.

Legacy Systems

Integration with existing legacy systems and customer databases posed significant technical challenges.

Our Solution

Easy AI Labs implemented a comprehensive Contact Center AI solution powered by Google Cloud

1

Discovery & Strategy

We conducted a comprehensive analysis of NexusBank's existing contact center operations, customer journeys, and pain points. This included call recordings analysis, agent interviews, and customer feedback review.

Based on our findings, we developed a strategic roadmap for implementing AI solutions that would address their specific challenges while integrating with existing systems.

2

Virtual Agent Implementation

We developed a sophisticated Virtual Agent using Dialogflow CX that could handle a wide range of customer inquiries, including:

  • Account balance inquiries
  • Transaction history and details
  • Fund transfers and payments
  • Card activation and management
  • Branch and ATM location information
  • Frequently asked questions

The Virtual Agent was deployed across multiple channels, including web, mobile app, and messaging platforms.

3

Voice Bot Development

We implemented a sophisticated Voice Bot using Google's speech recognition and natural language understanding technologies. The Voice Bot was designed to:

  • Handle inbound calls 24/7
  • Authenticate customers securely using voice biometrics
  • Understand natural language requests in multiple languages
  • Provide human-like responses with natural text-to-speech
  • Seamlessly transfer to human agents when needed, with full context
4

System Integration & Agent Assist

We integrated the AI solutions with NexusBank's existing systems, including:

  • Core banking system for real-time account information
  • Customer relationship management (CRM) system
  • Knowledge base for consistent information across channels
  • Existing telephony infrastructure

We also implemented Agent Assist features to help human agents handle complex inquiries more efficiently by providing real-time recommendations and information.

5

Analytics & Continuous Improvement

We set up comprehensive analytics dashboards to monitor performance, identify areas for improvement, and drive continuous optimization. This included:

  • Conversation analytics to identify common customer inquiries
  • Sentiment analysis to gauge customer satisfaction
  • Resolution rate tracking across channels
  • Regular model retraining based on new data

The Results

Transformative business outcomes achieved within 6 months of implementation
