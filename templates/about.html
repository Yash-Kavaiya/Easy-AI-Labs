About Easy AI Labs

We're on a mission to transform contact centers with cutting-edge AI solutions powered by Google Cloud technology.

Our Story

From vision to leading AI solutions provider

2020

The Beginning

Easy AI Labs was founded with a vision to make advanced AI accessible to businesses of all sizes. Our founders saw the potential for AI to transform customer service but noticed that many businesses struggled with implementation.

2021

Google Cloud Partnership

We became an official Google Cloud Partner, specializing in Contact Center AI solutions. This partnership allowed us to leverage Google's cutting-edge AI technologies to create powerful, scalable solutions for our clients.

2022

Rapid Growth

Our team expanded to 50+ AI experts, developers, and customer experience specialists. We successfully delivered over 100 AI implementation projects across various industries, from finance to healthcare.

2023

Innovation Hub

We established our AI Innovation Lab, dedicated to researching and developing new ways to apply conversational AI in business contexts. This initiative led to several breakthrough features in our solutions.

2025

Today

Today, Easy AI Labs is recognized as a leader in Contact Center AI solutions, helping businesses across the globe transform their customer experience with intelligent, conversational AI powered by Google Cloud.

Our Mission

To empower businesses with accessible, intelligent AI solutions that transform customer experiences, streamline operations, and drive meaningful business outcomes.

We believe that every business, regardless of size, should have access to the power of conversational AI to enhance their customer service capabilities.
