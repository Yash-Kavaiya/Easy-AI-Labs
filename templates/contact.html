Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and solutions

How long does it take to implement a Contact Center AI solution?

Implementation timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple chatbot deployments can take as little as 2-4 weeks, while full enterprise solutions might take 2-3 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a customized timeline based on your specific requirements.

Do you offer custom solutions or only pre-built packages?

We offer both pre-built solutions for rapid deployment and fully customized solutions tailored to your specific business needs. Our team works closely with you to understand your requirements and recommend the best approach for your organization.

Can your solutions integrate with our existing systems?

Yes, our solutions are designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing CRM, ticketing systems, knowledge bases, and other business applications. We have experience integrating with major platforms like Salesforce, Zendesk, ServiceNow, and many others.

What languages do your AI solutions support?

Our solutions leverage Google Cloud's language capabilities, supporting over 30 languages for text interactions and 20+ languages for voice interactions. This includes major languages like English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Chinese, and many more.

Do you provide ongoing support after implementation?

Absolutely. We offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages to ensure your AI solutions continue to perform optimally. This includes monitoring, regular updates, performance optimization, and continuous improvement based on conversation analytics.
